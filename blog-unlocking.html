Unlocking the Source of Darkness

By Meli Pil, MHC-LP, Mental Health Counselor, Limited Permit: IN PERSON SESSIONS ONLY

The emotional pain we experience—sadness, anxiety, shame, confusion, emptiness, and numbness—is not random. It is the result of unmet emotional needs and deeply ingrained patterns called Schemas.

5 Essential Emotional Needs

  • Safety: To feel protected and nurtured.
  • Autonomy: To feel capable of independence.
  • Expression: To voice needs without fear.
  • Spontaneity: To experience joy and play.
  • Limits: To have healthy boundaries.

The 18 Maladaptive Schemas

Abandonment: Fear of loss.
Mistrust: Expectation of harm.
Emotional Deprivation: Feeling unloved.
Defectiveness: Feeling flawed.
Social Isolation: Not belonging.
Failure: Feeling inadequate.
Subjugation: Giving up control.
Unrelenting Standards: High rules.
Self-Sacrifice: Neglecting self.
Punitiveness: Wanting punishment.

Understanding these schemas is the light that begins to dissolve the darkness.
