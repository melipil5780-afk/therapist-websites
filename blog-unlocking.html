Unlocking the Source of Darkness

By Meli Pil, MHC-LP, Mental Health Counselor, Limited Permit: IN PERSON SESSIONS ONLY

The emotional pain we experience—sadness, anxiety, shame, and emptiness—is not random. It is the result of unmet emotional needs and Schemas.

5 Essential Emotional Needs

  • Safety & Security: To feel protected and stable.
  • Autonomy: To feel independent and capable.
  • Expression: To voice needs without fear of rejection.
  • Play: To experience joy and spontaneity.
  • Limits: To have guidance and self-control.

The 18 Maladaptive Schemas

Abandonment: Belief that others will leave.
Mistrust: Expectation of being hurt.
Emotional Deprivation: Feeling unlovable.
Defectiveness: Feeling inwardly flawed.
Social Isolation: Feeling you don't belong.
Failure: Belief that you are inadequate.
Subjugation: Surrendering to others' control.
Unrelenting Standards: Striving for perfection.
Self-Sacrifice: Prioritizing others always.
Punitiveness: Wanting to punish mistakes.

Understanding this source is the first step toward the light.
