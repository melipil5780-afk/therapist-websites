THE APPROACH

A Journey of Discovery

My approach to therapy is collaborative and rooted in exploration. Rather than simply identifying problems and prescribing solutions, I work with my clients to help them uncover and understand their inner worlds.

Therapy, to me, is an emotional journey—an opportunity to name, process, and make sense of feelings that may have been difficult to access or articulate before.

By examining recurring patterns, clients can begin to shift and move toward a life more aligned with their authentic selves. I view each session as a collaborative process, ensuring that any ideas or strategies we explore feel true and helpful.

What you can expect

Safety & Pace

Your story unfolds at your own pace. It’s important that you feel safe and comfortable before diving into deeper layers of your experience.

Naming Emotions

We’ll name emotions never fully acknowledged and recognize parts of yourself that may have gone unnoticed or been in conflict.

Breaking Patterns

Using conversation, chair work, and relational dynamics to bring repeating patterns into focus and move toward intentional choices.

Authentic Self

Confronting difficult parts with compassion, allowing you to explore why you feel stuck and how to align with your values.
