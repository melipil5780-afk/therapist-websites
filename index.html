01 Arrival at the Threshold

Stand before the glowing gateway. Here, we begin not with words alone, but with presence. Feel your breath, your body, the ground beneath you. This is where emotional change starts — in the lived experience of now.

“Healing begins when we fully inhabit the moment.”

02 Relational Chair Work

In this quiet sanctuary, two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you, a loved one, or an inner voice. Speak. Listen. Switch places. Embody each side. Feel the unspoken become alive in your body and voice.

“We heal in relationship — even when the other is imagined.”

03 Releasing Through Movement

Along this ancient path, energy moves. Anger, grief, fear — they live in the body. Strike cushions, push against walls, stomp, shake, roar. Let the stuck emotion flow out through action. The body speaks what words have held back.

“What is held in the body must be moved to be freed.”

04 Sensing the Shift

After the wave of movement, pause. Feel what remains. Where does the energy live now? Notice heat, tingling, spaciousness, or tears. The body has shifted. Stay with the sensation — this is where transformation deepens.

“Awareness of the body is the bridge to lasting change.”

05 Giving Voice to the Experience

Now, speak. Name what moved through you. “I felt rage in my chest… then release in my arms.” Connect sensation to meaning. Reflect. Integrate. The emotional flood becomes a story you can carry forward with wisdom.

“Words after movement turn raw energy into insight.”

06 Living the Change

You emerge different. The body remembers. The mind understands. Together, they guide new choices. This is experiential therapy — not just talking about change, but living it through movement, relation, and reflection.
