Meet Meli

My approach to therapy is collaborative and rooted in exploration. Rather than simply identifying problems, I work with my clients to help them uncover and understand their inner worlds.

Therapy is an emotional journey—an opportunity to name and make sense of feelings that may have been difficult to articulate before. Together, we create a space where emotions are symbolized and transformed.
