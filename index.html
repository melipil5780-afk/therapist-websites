Experiential Psychotherapy

The Approach

I help people with a variety of presentations—whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, or simply confused. My work is about understanding how emotions play out in your body to transform distress into growth.

01 Arrival

Stand before the threshold. Feel your breath, your body, and the ground beneath you.

02 Chair Work

Two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you or a loved one.

03 Movement

Anger, grief, fear — they live in the body. Strike cushions, push against walls, stomp, shake, roar.

04 Sensing

Pause. Feel what remains. Notice the heat, the tingling, the spaciousness.

05 Voice

Name what moved. Connect sensation to meaning and turn raw energy into insight.

06 Integration

Carry the shift into your world. This is not just a session; it is a new way of being.
