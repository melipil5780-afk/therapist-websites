The Guide

My approach is collaborative and rooted in exploration. Rather than simply identifying problems and prescribing solutions, I work with my clients to help them uncover and understand their inner worlds.

Therapy, to me, is an emotional journey—an opportunity to name, process, and make sense of feelings that may have been difficult to access before.

The Live Encounter

I incorporate both conversation and experiential exercises to help clients engage with their emotions more deeply. This includes addressing aspects of emotional experience that may have been avoided, denied, or projected onto others.

By examining recurring patterns, we begin to shift and move toward a life more aligned with your authentic values.
