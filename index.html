Meet Meli

My approach to therapy is collaborative and rooted in exploration. Rather than simply identifying problems and prescribing solutions, I work with my clients to help them uncover and understand their inner worlds.

I incorporate both conversation and experiential exercises to help clients engage with their emotions more deeply. This includes addressing aspects of emotional experience that may have been avoided or denied.

The Somatic Chair

By using a range of approaches such as conversation, chair work, and an exploration of relational dynamics, we bring recurring patterns into focus.

You can expect a space where your story unfolds at your own pace. Together, we will explore your emotions within the framework of your thoughts, beliefs, values, and needs.
