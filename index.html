The Perspective

A Person is Not a Diagnosis.

My journey through inpatient clinics and psychiatric rehabilitation has brought me face-to-face with every presentation of human suffering—from the shadows of schizophrenia and bipolar experiences to the heavy silences of trauma and personality disorders.

I have learned that while a diagnosis provides a symptom map, it rarely reveals the person. I focus on the personality—the intricate patterns, the deep desires, and the unique way you make sense of the world.

Clinically Grounded

Experience in psych-rehab and acute clinical settings ensures that no presentation is too "heavy" or too "complex" for our space.

Relationally Driven

We work experientially to understand your themes and build a therapeutic alliance that achieves your specific goals.
