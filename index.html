The Approach

I help people with a variety of presentations—whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, or simply confused by the recurring patterns in your life. My work isn't just about identifying problems; it's about understanding how emotions play out in your body. We look at core vulnerabilities to transform distress into growth.

01 Arrival

Stand before the threshold. Here, we begin not with words alone, but with presence. Feel your breath, your body, and the ground beneath you. You are not walking this path alone.

“Healing begins when we fully inhabit the moment.”

02 Chair Work

In this quiet sanctuary, two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you, a loved one, or an inner voice. We bridge the gap between what is said and what is truly felt deep within.

“We heal in relationship — even when the other is imagined.”

03 Movement

Anger, grief, fear — they live in the body. Strike cushions, push against walls, stomp, shake, roar. What is held must be moved to be freed. We turn stagnant energy into purposeful action.
