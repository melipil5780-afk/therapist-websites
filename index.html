EXPERIENTIAL PSYCHOTHERAPY

The Approach

I help people with a variety of presentations—whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, or simply confused by the recurring patterns in your life. My work isn't just about identifying problems; it's about understanding how emotions play out in your body.

01 Arrival

Stand before the threshold. Here, we begin not with words alone, but with presence. Feel your breath, your body, and the ground beneath you.

“Healing begins when we fully inhabit the moment.”

02 Chair Work

In this quiet sanctuary, two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you, a loved one, or an inner voice.

“We heal in relationship.”

03 Movement

Anger, grief, fear — they live in the body. Strike cushions, push against walls, stomp, shake, roar. What is held must be moved to be freed.

04 Sensing

Pause. Feel what remains. Notice the heat, the tingling, the spaciousness.

05 Voice

Name what moved. Connect sensation to meaning and turn raw energy into insight.
