The Guide

My approach is collaborative and rooted in exploration. Rather than simply identifying problems and prescribing solutions, I work with my clients to help them uncover and understand their inner worlds.


Therapy is an opportunity to name, process, and make sense of feelings that have been difficult to access. Together, we create a space for curiosity and transformation.

The Live Encounter

I incorporate conversation and experiential exercises to help you engage with emotions more deeply. This includes addressing aspects of emotional experience that may have been avoided, denied, or projected onto others.


By examining recurring patterns in the here-and-now, we shift toward a life more aligned with your authentic values.
