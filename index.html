The Approach

I help people with a variety of presentations—whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, or simply confused by recurring patterns.

My work involves understanding how emotions play out in your body. We find primary emotions that transform distress into growth.

01 Arrival

Stand before the threshold. Here, we begin not with words alone, but with presence. Feel your breath, your body, and the ground beneath you.

“Healing begins when we fully inhabit the moment.”

02 Chair Work

Two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you, a loved one, or an inner voice.

“We heal in relationship — even when the other is imagined.”

03 Movement

Anger, grief, and fear live in the body. Strike cushions, push against walls, or roar. What is held must be moved to be freed.

“Motion releases what the mind cannot speak.”

04 Sensing

After movement, we pause. Where does the energy live now? Notice the tingling, the spaciousness, or the quiet tears.

“Awareness of the body is the bridge to lasting change.”

05 Voice

Name what moved through you. Connect the sensation to your personal meaning, turning raw energy into deep insight.

“Words turn somatic experience into wisdom.”
