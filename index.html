The Approach

I help people with a variety of presentations—whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, or simply confused. My work is about understanding how emotions play out in your body to transform distress into growth.

01 Arrival

Stand before the threshold. Here, we begin with presence. Feel your breath and the ground beneath you.

02 Chair Work

Two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you or an inner voice. We bridge the gap between words and felt truth.

03 Movement

Anger, grief, fear — they live in the body. What is held must be moved to be freed. We turn stagnant energy into purposeful action.
