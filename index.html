A Message From Meli

The Approach

I help people with a variety of presentations—whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, or simply confused by recurring patterns in your life.

My work isn't just about identifying problems; it's about understanding how emotions play out in your body. We look at core vulnerabilities and find primary emotions that transform distress into growth.

01 Arrival

Stand before the threshold. Here, we begin not with words alone, but with presence. Feel your breath, your body, the ground beneath you.

“Healing begins when we fully inhabit the moment.”

02 Relational Chair Work

In this quiet sanctuary, two chairs face each other. One holds you. The other holds a part of you, a loved one, or an inner voice.

“We heal in relationship — even when the other is imagined.”

03 Releasing Through Movement

Along this ancient path, energy moves. Anger, grief, fear — they live in the body. Strike cushions, push against walls, stomp, shake, roar.

“What is held in the body must be moved to be freed.”

04 Sensing the Shift

After the wave of movement, pause. Feel what remains. Where does the energy live now? Notice heat, tingling, spaciousness, or tears.

“Awareness of the body is the bridge to lasting change.”

05 Giving Voice

Now, speak. Name what moved through you. “I felt rage in my chest… then release in my arms.” Connect sensation to meaning.

“Words after movement turn raw energy into insight.”

06 Living the Change

You emerge different. The body remembers. The mind understands. Together, they guide new choices.
