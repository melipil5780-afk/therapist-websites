6 Principles for Working With Emotions

By Meli Pil, MHC-LP | Brooklyn, NY | In-Person Sessions Only

1. Awareness

Move from "being" the emotion to observing it. This creates the space necessary for processing.

2. Expression

Identify primary emotions rather than just secondary ones to ensure the root feeling is moved.

3. Regulation

Manage the intensity of feelings so they don't become overwhelming during the healing process.

4. Reflection

Determine what the emotion is telling you about your needs and boundaries.

5. Transformation

Using healthy emotions (like self-compassion or healthy anger) to shift stuck, painful ones.

6. Corrective Experience

Healing through a compassionate, real-time connection in the therapy room.
