6 Principles For Working With Emotions

Meli Pil, MHC-LP | Brooklyn, NY

1. Awareness

Instead of being the feeling one is perceiving, acknowledge the emotion. Witnessing the feeling allows you to separate from it.

2. Expression

Allow the feeling to be verbalized and expressed. However, if one only expresses secondary emotions, the primary emotion will not be properly processed.

3. Regulation

Bring down the intensity of the emotion to a level where one can actually process it.

4. Reflection

Make sense of emotions and understand patterns. What is the feeling telling you?
