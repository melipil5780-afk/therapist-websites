01

Awareness

The first step is moving from "being" the emotion to observing it. Awareness allows you to feel the physical sensation of an emotion in your body without being consumed by it.

02

Expression

Authentic expression is about finding the primary emotion (like loneliness or shame) hidden beneath secondary reactions like anger. We symbolize these feelings through words, imagery, or chair work.

03

Regulation

Regulation isn't about stopping a feeling; it's about keeping the intensity within your "window of tolerance" so that the work remains safe and healing.

04

Reflection

Every emotion is a messenger. We ask: "What is this feeling telling me about my needs, my boundaries, or my values?"

05

Transformation

We use healthy emotional states (like self-compassion) to transform stuck, maladaptive feelings. We replace old "Inner Blueprints" with new, adaptive emotional experiences.

06

Corrective Experience

By exploring the "here and now" in our session, we create real-time experiences of safety and connection that counteract the neglect or trauma of the past.
